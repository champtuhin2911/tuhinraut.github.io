About Me

As a research enthusiast at the intersection of bioinformatics and biotechnology, I'm passionate about learning and implementing computational methods to address complex biological challenges. My work spans molecular dynamics simulations, synthetic biology, and machine learning applications in genomics and proteomics. I have received grants for my work on Nanopores and Biosurfactants from Emergent Ventures (Mercatus Center) and Impact Grants (iGEM).

Presently I am pursuing my Bachelor's Degree from Indian institute of Technology, Delhi from the Department of Biochemical Engineering and Biotechnology.

Along side my love for biology, I am a tech nerd with a finger on the pulse of computing innovations, equally at home among nature's wonders or headbanging to metal's powerful rhythms.
